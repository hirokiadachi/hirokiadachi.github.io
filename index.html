Hiroki Adachi

    Email: ha618[at]mprg.cs.chubu.ac.jp

My name is Hiroki Adachi who studies computer vision, machine learning, and deep learning.
I'm a Ph.D. student in Robotics Science and Technology at Chubu University, Japan.
I am currently a member of Fujiyoshi and Yamashita Lab. a.k.a. Machine Perception and Robotics Group (MPRG) in Chubu University.
My research interests are generative model (GANs and VAE), monocular depth estimation, and adversarial attack/defense (mainly adversarial training).

  • Programming Languages: Python, C/C++, MATLAB, ROS
  • Skills: Deep learning, Computer vision, Linear algebra
