About me

I'm Hiroki Adachi, and I study computer vision and machine learning. I'm a MS student in Robotics Science and Technology at Chubu University. Currently, I'm a member of Fujiyoshi and Yamashita Lab where is Machine Perception and Robotics Group (MPRG). My research interests are generative model (GANs and VAE) and depth estimation.
